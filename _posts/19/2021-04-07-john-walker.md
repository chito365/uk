## Who is John Walker



Former middle-distance runner from New Zealand who won a gold medal in the 1976 Montreal Olympic Games 1500m.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He participated in sports during high school.

                
                
                
## Random data



He set a world record in Sweden for fastest time per mile, becoming the first person to run it in under 3:50.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Walker



He fathered four children with his wife, Helen.
