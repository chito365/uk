                    About Pat Rice
                  </h4>
                  
                  <p>

                  </p>
                </div>
                
                <div class="article_content">
                  <h4>
                    Early life
                  </h4>

                  </p>
                </div>
                
                <div class="article_content">
                  <h4>
                    Trivia
