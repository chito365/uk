#  About Amy Heidemann
                  
                  
                  
American pop singer known for being one half of the group Karmin with husband Nick Noonan.
                  
                
                
                
# Early life
                  
                  
                  
She attended the Berklee College of Music where she met Nick Noonan.
                  
                
                
                
# Trivia
